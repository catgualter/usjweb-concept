The University of Saint Joseph offers a wide range of academic programs at the Pre-University, undergraduate and post-graduate level, as well as non-degree programs, offered through its Lifelong Learning Office.The government of Macau accredits all our programs, and our graduates have been able to pursue careers and further studies with prestigious local and international institutions, thus endowing us with a high level of recognition and credibility.

Come and visit USJ any given day and you are likely to find a concert, debate, public lecture or other academic and cultural activity. Beside strong academic programs, being as student at USJ is an enriching involving experience that enables you to develp multiple skills and interests.

Pre-University

The Pre-University program of studies is intended to act as a bridge between secondary education and undergraduate studies, thus enabling students to meet the admissionâ€™s requirements to undergraduate programs at USJ and other universities that use English as the medium of instruction. It also prepares students for the self-propelling, autonomous and group-based, and interactive forms of study that are characteristic of many university programs.

Faculty of Christian Studies

Licentiate

Christian Studies

An innovative program of study for both those seeking ordination in the Catholic Church, or those wishing to make a commitment to the wider ministry of the Church and the improvement of society.

Master

Religious Studies

An inter-disciplinary program, providing professional, academic guidance, and on site field work in the different Macau religious heritage spots, as well as in historical archives, libraries and museums.

Doctorate

PhD. in Religious Studies

Students complete main modules in research methods and foundation knowledge in their proposed research field, and then work under a supervisor to complete their doctoral thesis.
